About the Programme


The SNPL programme enables qualifying students to access funded education immediately, with structured repayment bginning only after securing employment. The goal is to remove financial barriers to entry for high-potential learners.



Key Benefits

  • Zero upfront tuition fees for ccepted applicants
  • Affordable income-based repayments that only kick in once you are employed
  • Debt waived entirely if employment is not secured within 24 months of graduation
  • Access to mentorship, career coaching, and job placement support
  • Flexible repaayment pause available during qualifying life events
